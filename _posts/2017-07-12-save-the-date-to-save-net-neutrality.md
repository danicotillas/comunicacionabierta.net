Expect your internet connection to slow down on <strong>July 12th</strong>. This is what <strong>Battle for the Net</strong> is declaring on their website in response to <strong>Ajit Pai</strong>’s proposal of <strong>reversing Title II</strong>. Title II is a section in the Communications Act that ensures the government monitors all ISPs. But taking that away would mean internet providers could <strong>block or slow down whatever websites they choose</strong>. This could potentially cause a lot of websites to lose traffic and revenue, and it <strong>changes the freedom of the internet</strong>. As a protest, websites are going to slow down their loading time to demonstrate what it would be like if we reverse Title II. To make this confusing topic clear, we created this awesome infographic to help spread the word on net neutrality.
